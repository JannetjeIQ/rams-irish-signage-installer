# Competency Matrix: Ireland

Certifications expected of fitters on Irish signage installation work. Used by the specialist when populating Section 5 of the RAMS and when validating fitter selection for a job.

The competency tracker (per `your-contractor-profile.md` §6) is the source of truth on who currently holds what.

## Core certifications

| Certification | When required | Issuing body | Renewal cycle |
|---------------|---------------|--------------|---------------|
| **Safe Pass** | Any ROI construction site under S.I. 291/2013 | SOLAS | 4 years |
| **Manual Handling** | Every fitter (general duty under S.I. 299/2007) | Accredited trainer | 3 years (recommended) |
| **Working at Heights** | Any work above 2m or any ladder work | Accredited trainer | 3 years |
| **First Aid Responder (FAR)** | At least one fitter on site | PHECC accredited | 2 years |

## Access equipment certifications

| Certification | Covers | Issuing body | Renewal |
|---------------|--------|--------------|---------|
| **IPAF MEWP 1a** | Static vertical MEWP (mast climber, push-around) | IPAF (verified via ePAL) | 5 years |
| **IPAF MEWP 1b** | Static boom MEWP | IPAF (ePAL) | 5 years |
| **IPAF MEWP 3a** | Mobile vertical MEWP (scissor lift) | IPAF (ePAL) | 5 years |
| **IPAF MEWP 3b** | Mobile boom MEWP (cherry picker) | IPAF (ePAL) | 5 years |
| **PASMA** | Mobile tower assembly and use | PASMA | 5 years |

The fitter's IPAF licence must match the category of MEWP being used. A 3a operator cannot operate a 3b without the additional category.

## Specialist certifications (job-dependent)

| Certification | When required |
|---------------|---------------|
| **Abrasive Wheels** | Any cutting with abrasive discs (rare for signage; common for civils-adjacent work) |
| **Confined Space Entry** | Entry into ceiling voids, lift shafts, plant rooms, tanks |
| **Asbestos Awareness** | Any work in pre-2000 buildings where asbestos containing materials may be present |
| **Roofing competence** | Roof-top signage, plant deck installs |

## UK / NI substitutions

When working on a UK or NI site, swap as follows:

| ROI | UK / NI |
|-----|---------|
| Safe Pass (SOLAS) | CSCS card (CITB), 5-year renewal |
| HSA framework | HSE / CDM 2015 |
| FAR (PHECC) | First Aid at Work (FAA accredited), 3 years |
| S.I. 291/2013 | CDM 2015 Regulation 13 (Principal Contractor) and 15 (Contractor) |

IPAF and PASMA are recognised in both jurisdictions.

## Verification

For PSCS / main contractor scrutiny, the specialist will populate Section 5 with:

- Card number (where applicable, e.g., Safe Pass card number)
- Expiry date
- Name of the holder

If the contractor profile notes that the tracker is held by a named person ("on file with [tracker maintainer], last verified [date]"), that wording is acceptable on the RAMS itself, with the actual certificates available as Appendix G on request.

## What expires when

The specialist warns the user if any named fitter has a certification expiring within 60 days of the planned install date. The reasoning: a fitter you book today for a job in three weeks might be off cover by then.

The maintainer named in the contractor profile §6 is responsible for the 60-day-before-expiry alert process.
